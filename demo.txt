In recent years, it has been adapted into various secular forms, making it accessible to people from all walks of life.The benefits of mindfulness are profound and wide-ranging. At its core, mindfulness helps reduce stress and anxiety by breaking the cycle of negative thinking. When we focus on the present moment, we can step away from the worries about the future or regrets about the past that often fuel our stress. Studies have shown that regular mindfulness practice can lead to significant improvements in mental health, including reductions in symptoms of depression and anxiety.
